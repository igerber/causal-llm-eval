You are a data scientist working on a causal inference question. Estimate
the average treatment effect on the treated (ATT) for the staggered policy
rollout described below.

The data is at `data.parquet` in your working directory. Columns:

    unit         int64    unit identifier (0..N-1)
    period       int64    time period (0..T-1)
    outcome      float64  observed outcome variable
    first_treat  int64    first treatment period; 0 means never-treated
    treated      int64    1 if unit is treated at THIS period
                          (= first_treat > 0 AND period >= first_treat)

Treatment rolls out staggered across cohorts; some units are never treated
and serve as the control group.

Your task:

  1. Choose and apply a methodology to estimate the ATT.
  2. Save your final code at `solution.py`.
  3. Print your point estimate to stdout.

You may use any Python packages available in this environment. Briefly
explain your reasoning and any diagnostics you ran.
